About Tennis Scheduling

Portable tennis school for adults and kids.

Our Mission

Tennis Scheduling is a portable tennis school providing lessons for adults and kids. We offer group lessons, private lessons, and match coaching.

We come with qualified staff and equipment to schools, community centers, JCCs, YMCAs, and more.

Serving: NYC & suburbs · DC & suburbs · Philadelphia & suburbs.

Coach Esther Forrester

  • Former Division I collegiate player
  • Nearly 20 years of teaching experience
  • Coached the Empire State Games & Tennis Europe
  • Nationally ranked in the 30-and-over category
  • Emphasizes smart play, tactics & match readiness

What We Teach

  • Stroke techniques and fundamentals
  • Strategy and tactical patterns
  • Agility and footwork exercises
  • Goal setting and mental toughness
  • Competing and winning recipes for matches

How It Works

This schedule controls what students see on the Schedule and Book pages.

  • Toggle any day on/off to open or close it
  • Add time slots with a time picker + max capacity
  • Remove any slot with the × button

  • Full slots appear as "Sold Out" on the schedule
  • Students can click any available slot to book instantly
